Empire CAT History

Empire CAT is a leading provider of equipment, parts, and service solutions across Arizona. As the authorized Caterpillar (CAT) dealer for the region, we support a wide range of industries including construction, mining, forestry, power generation, and agriculture.

With decades of experience and a commitment to excellence, Empire CAT offers new and used equipment sales, rental services, comprehensive maintenance and repair, and advanced technology solutions.

At Empire CAT, we don't just sell machines - we build partnerships that power progress across the communities we serve.
