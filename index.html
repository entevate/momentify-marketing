05 — Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

Confident. Grounded. Operator-led. We sound like people who have been on the floor, in the booth, and in the room. Not theorists. Not hype merchants.

C
Confident
We speak with earned authority. We do not hedge, qualify, or over-explain. If a sentence does not generate value or insight, it does not exist.
G
Grounded
We deal in outcomes, not promises. Insight, not hype. Evidence, not guesses. We respect our audience's intelligence and their time.
O
Operator-Led
We write for doers. People who need to act, justify spend, and prove impact. Language should sound natural when spoken aloud.
Tone Spectrum
Calm urgency
Not pressure
Insightful
Not academic
Direct
Not aggressive
Human
Not corporate
Preferred Phrasing Patterns
"Most teams are busy. They are not blind by choice."
"The problem is not effort. It is visibility."
"What you do not measure is what you lose."
"Turn effort into evidence."
Internal Mantra
Turn effort into evidence.
Turn engagement into intelligence.
Turn moments into momentum.

✓ Do

  • Write short, declarative sentences with clear cause and effect
  • Lead with the problem, then the insight, then the value
  • Connect effort to outcomes: "See what happened. Know what to do next."
  • Write as if speaking to someone who has 90 seconds
  • Ask: does this help justify spend, act faster, or prove impact?

✗ Don't

  • Use em dashes. Ever.
  • Use startup clichés: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "disruptive"
  • Dump features without connecting them to outcomes
  • Use filler words: "very", "really", "just", "actually"
  • Write long explanations that have no clear payoff
06 — Usage

Logo Usage Rules

Consistent application of the logo builds trust and recognition. These rules are non-negotiable.

1

Clear Space

Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the "M" in the wordmark around all sides of the logo. Never crowd it with other elements.

2

Minimum Size

The full logo should never appear smaller than 120px wide in digital contexts, or 30mm wide in print. Below this, use the icon mark only.

3

Never Alter the Logo

Do not stretch, rotate, recolour, add effects, or recreate the logo. Always use the approved SVG files provided in this kit.

4

Background Contrast

Use the Primary logo on white or light surfaces. Use the Reverse (white) logo on dark, navy, or gradient backgrounds. Never place the logo on a busy photo without a solid backing.

5

No Unauthorised Lockups

Do not pair the Momentify logo with partner logos, icons, or taglines without explicit approval from the brand team.

6

Gradient Integrity

The icon's gradient must always run from Cyan (#0CF4DF) to Deep Blue (#1F3395). Do not swap gradient directions or substitute colours.
