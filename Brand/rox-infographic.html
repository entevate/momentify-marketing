Return on Experience (ROX)

ROI tells you what you spent.
ROX tells you whether it worked.

Return on Experience (ROX) is a proprietary measurement standard for in-person engagement. It scores the quality of every interaction, not just the count.

What ROX Measures
01
Lead Capture Efficiency
How many visitors became a real lead? A badge scan is not a lead. This measures the percentage captured with enough context to act on.
02
Engagement Quality
How deeply did visitors interact with your content, demos, or conversations? This separates genuine interest from casual passers-by.
03
Follow-Up Speed
Intent fades fast. Best-in-class teams follow up same day. The average delay is five to seven days, and by then the opportunity is gone.
04
Conversion Effectiveness
How many leads turned into a meeting, a hire, or a closed opportunity? This closes the loop between the event floor and outcomes.
